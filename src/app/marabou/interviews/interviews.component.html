Interview Guide


Welcome to the Marabou Interview Guide!

Interviews are a core part of our service. We believe strongly that a good interview is the foundation of a good journey, so we work hard to make our interview procedure fabulous.


We've created this comprehensive guide that has magic features to make it easy for you to use.


This procedure is managed by the Human Resources Department. So, if you have any questions, drop one of them a line.

Set

Host

It's incredibly important that the server is never slocked during the session. We don't slock because we operate a system we call 'Open Opportunity Interviews'. This means that everyone is able to join, regardless of it they popped along 20 minutes before, or 20 minutes after it started.


We believe that our systems are comprehensive enough to have it so we never slock, so we don't!
